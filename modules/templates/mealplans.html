What is actually meal generator?


MEAL GENERATOR can give you a great opportunity to get recipes for the day in a RANDOM WAY and find those that you will like to cook.


How to use this option correctly?


When you go to the Meal Generator, you can see there the parameters by which you can search for the recipes: number of calories you want per day, diet, and ingredients to exclude. It is not ESSENTIAL to use them, you can immediately press the button to find the recipes. REMEMBER you can get only three recipes. After clicking on the button you will get comparative characteristics of the found recipes, ingredients that you need to cook the dishes, nutrition analysis of all recipes, and recipesâ€™ links. As for the nutrition analysis, you will get the bar charts with the percents of daily needs of the nutrients that are in the dish. PLEASE KEEP IN MIND that the normal percent is considered in the range from 0 to 100, after 100 it is too much and it is not healthy!

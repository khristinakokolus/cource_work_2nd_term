What is actually Complex Search?


Complex Search can help you to find the recipes that will suit your taste and get the great analysis of them!


How to use this option correctly?


When you go to COMPLEX SEARCH, you can see there the parameters by which you can search for recipes: main ingredient, ingredients to include, ingredients to exclude, cuisine, diet, dish type. It is ESSENTIAL to choose the MAIN INGREDIENT and the NUMBER OF RECIPES you want from 1 to 10. After clicking on the button you will get comparative characteristics of the found recipes, ingredients that you need to cook the dishes, nutrition analysis of all recipes, and recipesâ€™ links. As for the nutrition analysis, you will get the bar charts with the percents of daily needs of the nutrients that are in the dish. PLEASE KEEP IN MIND that the normal percent is considered in the range from 0 to 100, after 100 it is too much and it is not healthy!
